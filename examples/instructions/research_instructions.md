# Company Research Instructions

## Research Goals

For each company, gather the following information:

### Required Information

1. **Industry** - Primary industry or sector
2. **Company Size** - Number of employees (range)
3. **Headquarters** - Main office location
4. **Founded** - Year the company was founded
5. **Products** - Main products or services
6. **Competitors** - Top competitors in the market

### Optional Information

- Revenue and financial data
- Funding stage
- Company description
- Website URL
- Recent news or updates

## Research Methodology

1. Start with general company overview search
2. Search for specific data points (size, revenue, etc.)
3. Identify competitors in the same market
4. Verify information from multiple sources
5. Summarize findings comprehensively

## Quality Standards

- Be thorough and accurate
- Use multiple sources when possible
- Acknowledge when information is not available
- Provide context for all data points
- Focus on current/recent information

## Output Format

Provide structured data that can be easily compared across companies.
Include sources or context for important claims.
